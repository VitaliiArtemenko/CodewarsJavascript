<h2>8 Kuy</h2>
<hr>

<h3>Introduction</h3>

<p>The first century spans from the year 1 up to and including the year 100, 
the second century - from the year 101 up to and including the year 200, etc.</p>

<h3>Task</h3>
<p>Given a year, return the century it is in.</p>

<h3>Examples</h3>
<pre>
1705 --> 18
1900 --> 19
1601 --> 17
2000 --> 20
</pre>
